Comparing Some of My Favorite Sites

The Rap Insider, Complex, and Genius (aka Rap Genius)

The Rap Insider

I love this site because of the hilarious content first and foremost...and I also think it is pretty good looking. I like its simple fonts and the uniformity of formal elements. I also like the red, white and black color scheme. I feel like color schemes that have a lot of secondary colors look cheesy because they clash with the photos. Having red just makes the photos pop and everything still seems to match.

At first glance my eye is drawn to the large slideshow photo on the top left. After stepping back, I think my eye is also drawn to the "Join TRI!" announcement and the logo on top.
